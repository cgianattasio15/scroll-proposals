Social SEO

Cast wide. Compress to OTR.

Instagram is a search engine now, and a local salon is found in search before it is found on a feed. We build the keyword ladder so Pippi's shows up for the right searches from day one, and we set the Instagram search foundation (profile name field, location tags, geo-tags) that a neighborhood business lives on.

Pippi's · 4-Tier TAM Compression Keyword Bank

Each tier compresses the total addressable market. Tier 1 is broad enough to spike algorithmic reach. Tier 4 is hyper-local and authority-anchored. Together they cover the full search journey from "hair inspo" to "best colorist in OTR."

Tier 1 · Niche-Broad (no geo) · Broad Reach Cast the widest net inside the niche. Spike the algorithm with broad relevance signal.
hair color balayage blonde specialist hair transformation hair colorist haircut inspo salon
Tier 2 · Niche + Demographic · Qualified Narrow by craft specialty and audience. Still pre-geo.
women's hair salon luxury hair color editorial hair color specialist premium salon lived-in color
Tier 3 · Metro · Cincinnati-Anchored Add geographic specificity. Cincinnati anchoring.
Cincinnati hair salon Cincinnati colorist Cincinnati balayage Cincinnati blonde Cincinnati hair
Tier 4 · Hyper-local + Authority · Max Intent Neighborhood specifics. These are the highest-intent search terms.
Over-the-Rhine salon OTR hair colorist Vine Street salon best colorist Cincinnati OTR balayage
Where These Land
  • Profile name field (high SEO weight)
  • Bio (descriptive copy, primary terms front-loaded)
  • Highlight covers and titles
  • Caption opening lines (Instagram weights the first sentence heavily)
  • Alt text on every post
  • Geo-tags on location-relevant posts

Why this matters. Tier 1 builds reach, Tier 4 captures the person ready to book a colorist in Over-the-Rhine tonight. The middle tiers are the bridge.
