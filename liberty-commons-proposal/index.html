Who we're reaching. How we'll reach them.

Two audiences, reverse-engineered from the two services that matter most. We do not build content for "people who need a dentist." We build it for the specific person considering a specific procedure, and every post knows which one it is talking to.

Adults, often 45 and up, living with failing, broken-down, or missing teeth, or stuck in dentures that never felt right. Frequently it is not the patient who starts the search. Motivators

  • Being done with temporary fixes

Current Challenges

  • The belief that they have "let it go too far"

Dream Outcomes

  • A new smile in a single day
  • Eating the foods they gave up
  • Walking into their next event looking and feeling like themselves again
  • The relief of having finally handled it
