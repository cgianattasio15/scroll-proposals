07
Why organic social

Over 70% of the world is on social media for an average of 2.5 hours per day. We've never had this level of consumer attention in a single marketing channel. Our strategy captures that attention for Pippi's, and gets you in front of the people who care most about the room you're building. When you do that well, booked chairs follow. You can't grow a salon without people knowing it exists. Organic social is where they're paying attention.

Strategic approachGetting found

Start broad to get found. Narrow to get chosen.

Reaching the right audience in Cincinnati is a three-step approach. Instagram only: search, location tags, and geo-tags, not Google.

Step 1

Test heavily

The first couple of months are about testing, not performance. We try a wide range of formats and topics to learn what resonates with the exact audience you want, watch the data, and double down on what's working. We are not chasing view counts early. We are learning the audience.

Step 2

Instagram search

Instagram is a search engine. It runs on the keywords your client types, not just hashtags. We build a keyword ladder so Pippi's surfaces for the right searches from day one, then narrow as the room fills. Keywords live in the name field, bio, highlight covers, caption openers, alt text, and geo-tags.

WidestBroad reachhair color · balayage · blonde specialist · hair transformation
NarrowThe right categorywomen's hair salon · lived-in color · editorial color · color correction
MetroThe geographyCincinnati colorist · Cincinnati balayage · Cincinnati blonde
Hyper-localThe exact searchOver-the-Rhine salon · OTR colorist · Vine Street salon · best colorist Cincinnati
Success axis: Month 1: reach → Month 6+: precision. The audience narrows while the quality rises, mirroring the content funnel.
Step 3

Strategic reach tactics

To spike a brand-new account into the algorithm, we layer three plays on top of consistent posting.

Warm local collaborationsWe broker warm collaborations with visible, social-active OTR businesses to co-create a "get the look" piece. The partner shares to their audience, and we anchor every collab on a moment your client also wants. Illustrative, not committed: a Vine Street boutique like Idlewild, a food and coffee spot like Mom n Em for a custom tea blend, and a non-competing beauty studio.
Ignition + communitySeed from your owned network day one: your following, your book, OTR relationships. About 30 minutes a day of real engagement on the exact accounts your audience follows, plus replying to every comment and DM.
Launch momentsCapture the friends-and-family party, the grand opening, and any mentor visits with notice. Real moments spike a new account and give the grid its first proof.
