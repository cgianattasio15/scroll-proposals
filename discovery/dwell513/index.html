Agenda: We'll spend about 20 minutes on these questions, then walk you through investment options and next steps. Feel free to jump around — this is a conversation, not an interview.
Your Business & Goals
  • What's driving you to focus on social media right now — is there a specific goal or moment that made this a priority?
    Listen for: urgency, specific trigger, timeline pressure
  • Are you primarily focused on seller leads, buyer leads, or building overall brand authority in Cincinnati?
    Listen for: which side of the transaction is more valuable to them
  • Is there a specific neighborhood, price point, or buyer/seller profile you want to dominate?
    Listen for: niche focus — luxury, first-time buyers, OTR, Hyde Park, NKY, etc.
Current Situation & Pain Points
  • You've posted 714 times on Instagram — what's been the biggest frustration with how it's performed so far?
    Listen for: growth plateau, inconsistency, no leads, time drain
  • Have you worked with a social media agency or freelancer before? What worked, what didn't?
    Listen for: previous bad experiences, what they're trying to avoid
  • How much time are you currently spending on social media each week — and is that sustainable?
    Listen for: bandwidth constraints, desire to hand off completely
Content & Assets
  • Do you have a photographer or videographer you work with for listings, or do you rely on listing photos?

  • When a new listing goes live, how quickly do you typically need content ready to post?
    This is the real estate niche question — helps scope listing-responsive shoot needs
Fit & Investment
  • What does success look like for you at 6 months? What would need to be true for you to say "this was worth it"?
    Listen for: specific metrics, lead volume, brand recognition, time savings
