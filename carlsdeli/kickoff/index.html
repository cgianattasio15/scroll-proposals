Approvals in Plan

Two things protect the calendar: timing and clarity.

All content reviews and approvals run through Plan, our social media content calendar and planning tool. Here's the rhythm that keeps posts on schedule.

Approvals are required 5 business days before a post is scheduled to go live.

This is the single rule that keeps the calendar on track. Without approval in hand 5 business days out, the post either moves or doesn't publish.

STEP 01 · SCROLL MEDIA
Day 0
We deliver content to Plan for your review.
STEP 02 · CARL'S DELI
Within 2 business days
You leave feedback or edit requests directly in Plan.
STEP 03 · CARL'S DELI
5 business days before publish
Final approval in hand. Content locks and goes into the publish queue.
Revisions: Up to two rounds of edits per post or batch. The first 30 days of live content is a calibration window where revisions run more freely while we lock in voice and visual direction.

What keeps the calendar on track

  • All feedback in Plan, not over text or email
  • Edits consolidated into one round when possible
  • Sydney as the main point of contact for sign-off
  • Approvals 5 business days before publish, every time

What pushes the calendar back

  • Approvals arriving under the 5-day window
  • Conflicting feedback from multiple stakeholders
  • Feedback spread across Slack, email, and text
  • Revision requests beyond two rounds per batch
