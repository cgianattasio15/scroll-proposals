Discovery Questions

These are the questions we want to work through together today. The more honest you are, the better we can build for you. This is a conversation — not an interview.

Your Business & Goals
You mentioned your primary goal is generating more leads and inquiries. What does a "lead" look like for you — is it a phone call, a DM, a form submission? And what's the typical value of a closed deal?
Listen for: Helps frame the ROI conversation. One closed deal at $10K+ commission = months of service paid for.
Are you primarily focused on seller leads, buyer leads, or building overall brand authority in Cincinnati? Is there a specific neighborhood or price point you want to own?
Listen for: Niche focus — luxury, first-time buyers, OTR, Hyde Park, NKY, etc. Shapes IVP development.
You've been in business 10+ years and rebranded as DWELL513 in 2017. What's driven the most business for you historically — and why hasn't Instagram been part of that equation?
Listen for: Referral-heavy business, skepticism about social ROI, or just never had the right system.
Current Situation & Pain Points
You said you're posting regularly but not seeing meaningful results. What does "regularly" look like — how often, what types of content? And what result would make you feel like it's working?
Listen for: Defines their baseline and success criteria. Also reveals if they're posting without strategy.
You mentioned lack of marketing management and low engagement as your biggest bottlenecks. Is the issue bandwidth — not enough time to do it right — or is it more about not knowing what to do?
Listen for: Time vs. strategy gap. Shapes whether they need full handoff or a blueprint first.
Have you worked with a social media agency or freelancer before? What happened?
Listen for: Previous bad experiences, what they're trying to avoid, trust barriers to address.
Content & Assets
You mentioned Heart & Cru, Oyler Hines, and Finn Team as brand inspiration. What specifically do you like about how they show up on social — is it the aesthetic, the content types, the tone?
Listen for: Visual direction, lifestyle vs. listing balance, founder-forward vs. brand-forward.
When a new listing goes live, how quickly do you typically need content ready? Do you have a photographer you work with for listings, or do you rely on MLS photos?
Real estate niche question — scopes listing-responsive shoot needs and existing asset pipeline.
Fit & Investment
What does success look like for you at 6 months? What would need to be true for you to say "this was worth it"?
Listen for: Specific metrics, lead volume, brand recognition, time savings. Sets the proposal benchmark.
Our retainer engagements start at $2,750/month. I want to be upfront about that so we're both working with the right expectations. Does that range make sense for where you are right now?
Budget qualification — if it's a stretch, explore Blueprint as an entry point. If yes, move to Signature.
