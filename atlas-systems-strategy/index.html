07 Bottom of funnel · The lead engine
Bottom of funnel · Conversion

The lead engine.

A clean path from content to a booked fit call. The job of this stage is to generate qualified fit-call lead signal.

1 Content A post earns the right attention.
2 Comment “AUDIT” The viewer raises a hand.
3 Free readiness scorecard AI & Tech Readiness Scorecard, sent instantly.
4 A couple of DM questions Light qualifying, in the DM.
5 Booked fit call The founder takes it from here.
What the scorecard is
A fast, free self-assessment that shows an owner where their systems are leaking time. It is a lite version of the paid Technology Assessment. We respond in seconds (automation catches the lead), then the founder takes the real conversation fast.
Pillar 3 (Proof & Process) feeds this stage with before-and-afters and the Assessment explainer.
