The Pattern We See

Five reasons social media
stops working.

These aren't guesses. They're the same five things we see across every business that's tried social media and walked away frustrated.

01
Posting without a strategy
Content goes up when someone has time, not when the strategy calls for it. There's no system behind what gets posted, why it gets posted, or what it's supposed to do for the business. Presence without purpose doesn't convert.
02
No content funnel — no purchasing journey
Social media isn't a place to sell. It's a place to walk someone from not knowing you exist, to trusting you enough to reach out. Without a top, middle, and bottom of funnel strategy, you're skipping steps in the buyer's journey — and losing people before they ever get to the decision stage.
03
Zero Social SEO — invisible to the algorithm
Instagram is a search engine now. Captions, keywords, hashtags, and alt text all feed the algorithm's understanding of who your content is for. Without intentional Social SEO, your content only reaches people who already follow you — not the new buyers and sellers you're trying to reach.
04
No clear IVP — blending in with everyone else
Every real estate agent in Cincinnati is posting listings, market updates, and "just sold" posts. If your content looks like everyone else's, the algorithm treats it like everyone else's. Your Ideal Value Proposition — what makes DWELL513 different — needs to be the backbone of every piece of content you create.
05
Copying formats without understanding why they worked
Seeing a competitor's post perform well and recreating it sounds logical. But format alone isn't why content performs. The hook, the psychology, the audience signal — those are what drive results. Copying the surface without the strategy underneath it produces nothing.
What a Real System Looks Like

Three stages.
One clear path to leads.

Every piece of content we create maps to one of three stages. Together, they walk your viewer from discovering you to trusting you to reaching out.

TOFU
Top of Funnel
Awareness & Discovery
Get in front of people who don't know you yet. The goal here isn't to sell — it's to be discovered by the right buyers and sellers in Cincinnati who are actively looking for an agent they can trust.
For DWELL513: "5 Cincinnati neighborhoods most buyers overlook" — broad reach, local authority, no ask.
MOFU
Middle of Funnel
Education & Trust
Turn awareness into credibility. This is where 10+ years of market expertise becomes your biggest asset. Educational content, market insights, and behind-the-scenes process content build the trust that makes someone choose you over the next agent.
For DWELL513: "What I tell every first-time buyer before they make an offer" — positions Doug as the expert, not the salesperson.
BOFU
Bottom of Funnel
Action & Lead Gen
Convert trust into conversations. By the time someone reaches this stage, they already know you, like you, and trust you. The ask becomes natural — not a cold pitch, but the logical next step for someone who's been following your journey.
For DWELL513: "Thinking about buying or selling in 2026? Here's where to start." — direct CTA to a warm, already-interested audience.
