MOFU — Process + Payoff
Why It Performed
  • Format: Process Reel — ingredient reveal, step-by-step build, final product close-up
  • Hook: Specific ingredient combination + visual payoff = stops the scroll for food-interested viewers immediately
  • Why it works: Process content builds desire before the product is even available to buy. The viewer is sold on the craft before they ever see a price or a CTA.
Applied to Maggie's
  • Format: "The Crust Press Reel" — show the graham cracker base being pressed, the filling being piped, the flavor being finished. Let the process be the hook.
  • Hook: "This is how we make 400 mini cheesecakes for a corporate event." Scale + process = the most powerful trust-builder in the catering space.
  • Goal: MOFU content that builds desire and trust simultaneously — the foundation of the Scroll content system for Maggie's
BOFU — Comment Trigger / Lead Capture
Why It Performed
  • Format: Recipe/process Reel with a comment trigger CTA ("Comment RECIPE and I'll send it to you")
  • Hook: Useful, specific content + frictionless lead capture = comment volume that boosts algorithmic reach AND captures warm leads in DMs simultaneously
  • Why it works: The comment trigger mechanic drives engagement (which the algorithm rewards) while capturing leads (which the business needs). One post does both jobs.
Applied to Maggie's
  • Format: "Comment MENU and I'll send you our full flavor list" — or "Comment BOOK and I'll send you our catering availability." Every post becomes a lead capture tool.
  • Hook: "Planning an event in Cincinnati? Comment GUIDE and I'll send you exactly how many mini cheesecakes you need per guest."
  • Goal: BOFU conversion content that drives DMs and catering inquiries from warm viewers who are already interested
