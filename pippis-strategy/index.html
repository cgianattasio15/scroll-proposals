07
Why organic social

Over 70% of the world is on social media for an average of 2.5 hours per day. We've never had this level of consumer attention in a single marketing channel. Our strategy captures that attention for Pippi's, and gets you in front of the people who care most about the room you're building. When you do that well, booked chairs follow. You can't grow a salon without people knowing it exists. Organic social is where they're paying attention.

Strategic approachReaching them

How we reach the right audience.

A three-step approach, all on Instagram, search, location, and reach plays, never Google.

Step 1

Test heavily

The first two months are for testing, not performance. We try a wide range of formats to learn what your exact audience responds to, then double down.

Step 2

Instagram SEO

Instagram search runs on keywords. We load your name field, bio, and captions with them, broad to get found, narrow to the exact person.

WidestBroad reachhair color · balayage · blonde specialist
NarrowThe right categorylived-in color · editorial color · color correction
MetroThe geographyCincinnati colorist · Cincinnati balayage
Hyper-localThe exact searchOver-the-Rhine salon · Vine Street salon
Month 1: reach → Month 6+: precision. The audience narrows while the quality rises.
Step 3

Strategic reach plays

Three plays on top of consistent posting.

CollaborationsWarm local names we broker: Idlewild, Mom n Em. Their audience meets yours.
Proactive engagementAbout 30 minutes a day on the accounts your audience already follows.
Events and mentor visitsCaptured live, with notice, to spike a new account.
