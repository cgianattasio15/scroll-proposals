Built only for you

The All-on-X Transformation Story System. Your most ownable content, made repeatable.

An All-on-X case is an eight-hour day that ends with a person walking out with a brand-new smile. The last partner kept pushing it aside as "just surgical." It is not just surgical. It is the meat of your business and the most emotional story in dentistry. We build a repeatable system to capture it, clear it, and serialize it.

The shoot problem we solve. Shutting the office down to film costs you $17,000 to $20,000 a day. That is exactly why our shoots are prepped, scripted, and batched, with a 2-to-3-person crew that includes a woman on set every time. We capture full-arch days with at least a week of notice, working around the surgery, never in the way of it.

▼

3 · Serialize · one case, many posts
▼
