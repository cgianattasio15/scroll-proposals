05Goals

What success looks like.

We build a step-by-step purchasing journey for your ideal customer, turning a high-quality audience into real Pippi's clients.

Awareness · Top of funnelRelatable content
Build brand association and get the right OTR audience interested in Pippi's. Success: views and follows.
Trust and authority · MiddleEducational + authority
Build trust and authority so that audience engages with and believes in Pippi's. Success: saves, comments, and profile visits.
Conversion · Bottom of funnelProof content
Turn trust into consideration and action, so Pippi's audience clicks and books. Success: link taps and conversion rate.

The overarching goal: a high-quality Pippi's audience that turns into organic new customers, qualified leads, and real business growth.

How we track it Each metric gets a specific target range every month, set to your account's maturity stage, so a launching account is never measured against a mature one.
