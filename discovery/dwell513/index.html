Discovery Questions

What we want to
work through together.

These are the questions we've prepared based on what you shared. Think of this as a guide for the conversation — not a script. The goal is to get to the real picture fast.

01
Business & Goals
Understanding what success actually looks like
Ask
What does a "lead" actually look like for you — a DM, a call, a form submission? And what's the typical commission on a closed deal?
Listen For
One closed deal at $10K+ commission = months of retainer paid for. This anchors the ROI conversation and reframes the investment.
Ask
Are you focused on seller leads, buyer leads, or overall brand authority? Is there a specific neighborhood or price point you want to own?
Listen For
Niche focus shapes the entire Ideal Viewer Profile (IVP) and content strategy. Luxury vs. first-time buyers vs. OTR vs. NKY = completely different content systems.
Ask
DWELL513 has been around 10+ years. What's driven the most business historically — and why hasn't Instagram been a bigger part of that equation?
Listen For
Referral-heavy business, skepticism about social ROI, or just never had the right system. This is where the real objection lives — address it directly.
02
Current Situation & Pain Points
Diagnosing the real problem behind the symptoms
Ask
You said you're posting regularly but not seeing results. What does "regularly" look like — how often, what types of content? And what result would make you feel like it's working?
Listen For
Defines their baseline and success criteria. If they're posting without a strategy, that's the core problem. 714 posts with 3,803 followers confirms this — volume isn't the issue.
Ask
You mentioned lack of marketing management and low engagement as the biggest bottlenecks. Is the issue bandwidth — not enough time to do it right — or is it more about not knowing what to do?
Listen For
Time vs. strategy gap. If it's time → full handoff (Signature). If it's strategy → Blueprint as entry point. This determines the right offer to lead with in the proposal.
Ask
Have you worked with a social media agency or freelancer before? What happened?
Listen For
Previous bad experiences, what they're trying to avoid, trust barriers to address. This is often where the real objection lives — and where Scroll's strategy-first approach differentiates.
03
Content & Assets
What we're working with and what we need to build
Ask
You mentioned Oyler Hines, Finn Team, and Hart & Cru as inspiration. What specifically do you like about how they show up — the aesthetic, the content types, the tone, or something else?
Listen For
Visual direction, lifestyle vs. listing balance, founder-forward vs. brand-forward. This shapes the creative brief and the IVP for the proposal. Specificity here = better strategy.
Ask
When a new listing goes live, how quickly do you need content ready? Do you have a photographer you work with, or do you rely on MLS photos?
Listen For
Scopes listing-responsive shoot needs and existing asset pipeline. Real estate moves fast — we need to know if a flexible shoot structure is required in the proposal.
04
Fit & Investment
Making sure we're aligned before we build anything
Ask
What does success look like at 6 months? What would need to be true for you to say "this was worth it"?
Listen For
Specific metrics, lead volume, brand recognition, time savings. This becomes the benchmark for the proposal and the 6-month check-in. Vague answers = need to push for specifics.
Ask
Our retainer engagements start at $2,750/month. I want to be upfront about that so we're both working with the right expectations. Does that range make sense for where you are right now?
Listen For
If it's a stretch → explore Blueprint as entry point (credited toward Signature within 60 days). If yes → move straight to Signature in the proposal. Don't oversell the wrong tier.
